About StudySummarize

StudySummarize helps students turn long study content into clearer, shorter summaries. Right now it’s frontend-only (demo summarizer). Next step is connecting an AI API.

How it works

  1. Paste your text
  2. Choose length + format
  3. Generate & copy/download

What’s next

  • API integration (real AI summaries)
  • File upload (PDF/TXT)
  • Saved summaries (optional)
